About Us

Samaki Express EA Ltd is a leading aquaculture company dedicated to transforming fish farming in Kenya and beyond. We provide high-quality products, equipment, and expert guidance to help farmers succeed.

To provide quality aquaculture products and services that empower fish farmers to increase productivity and profitability.

To be the leading aquaculture solutions provider in East Africa, driving sustainable fish farming practices.
